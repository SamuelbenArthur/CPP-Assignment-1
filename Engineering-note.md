# Engineering Note

## Topic

Electrical Power Calculation

## Formula Used

P = V × I

## Meaning of Variables

* P = Power (Watts)
* V = Voltage (Volts)
* I = Current (Amperes)

## Why This Matters in Electrical/Electronics Engineering

Electrical power calculation is important because engineers use it to determine how much power a circuit, component, or device consumes. This helps in selecting appropriate power supplies, designing efficient systems, and preventing component overload.

## Sample Input

Voltage = 12 V

Current = 2 A

## Sample Output

Power = 24 W
